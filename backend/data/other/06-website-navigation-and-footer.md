# Asan Innovators — Website Navigation and Footer Structure

Asan Innovators is a technology company headquartered in Nizampet, Hyderabad, Telangana, India. This document describes the standard navigation and footer structure across every page of our website (asaninnovators.com).

Every page ends with a standard footer containing:

1. **Certifications & Partnerships section** — Startup India, MSME, and ISO 9001:2015 certification badges, with the caption "Recognised by national programmes and trusted technology partners."
2. **Company tagline** — "Building AI tools, apps, and digital growth systems for teams that want to move fast."
3. **Services navigation column**
4. **Company navigation column**
5. **Policies navigation column**
6. **"Get In Touch" contact block**
7. **"Start A Project" call-to-action button**
