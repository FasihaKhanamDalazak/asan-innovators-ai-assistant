## Description

LeadPilot is a unified intelligence system for sales-driven organisations. Every touchpoint — from the first impression to the final conversion — is connected, analyzed, and made smarter by AI (Artificial Intelligence).

## What Powers LeadPilot

1. **AI-native architecture** — Every workflow is powered by machine learning models trained on real sales data.
2. **Multi-language support** — All portals and workflows are available in multiple languages.
3. **End-to-end sales intelligence** — Every step from first touch to close is tracked and optimized.
4. **Enterprise-grade security** — ISO-aligned data handling, role-based access, and audit trails.
5. **Multi-portal architecture** — Founder, Ad Manager, and Telecaller portals are combined in one system.
6. **Real-time automation** — Instant lead routing, follow-up triggers, and smart nudges.

## LeadPilot's Three Portals

"Three Portals, One System":

### 1. Founder Portal
A high-level command center providing pipeline health, team performance, and revenue insights in one view. Intended for business founders/owners overseeing overall sales performance.

### 2. Ad Manager Portal
Allows management of campaigns, creatives, and spend from one AI-monitored hub. Shows what converts and why. Intended for marketing/advertising managers.

### 3. Telecaller Portal
Provides real-time call scoring, conversation guides, and lead follow-up automation. Intended for frontline telesales/telecalling staff.
