# Asan Innovators — Upcoming Products (Automation Studio and Growth Dashboard)

Asan Innovators is a technology company headquartered in Nizampet, Hyderabad, Telangana, India. In addition to our flagship product LeadPilot, we have two further upcoming products, both listed under "In The Pipeline — What's coming next."

"We build products we believe in deeply and release them only when they're ready."

## Automation Studio

- **Status:** Coming Soon
- **Description:** A visual workflow builder for business operations — "drag, connect, and deploy in minutes."

**Features:**
1. Drag-and-drop workflow canvas
2. 100+ pre-built automation blocks
3. CRM (Customer Relationship Management) and API connectors
4. Real-time error monitoring

Automation Studio is a self-service, in-house counterpart to our custom Automation service, letting businesses build simpler automations themselves without engaging our team directly for every workflow.

## Growth Dashboard

- **Status:** Coming Soon
- **Description:** Unified analytics across every marketing channel, from ad spend to organic reach.

**Features:**
1. Cross-channel attribution
2. Live ROI (Return on Investment) tracking
3. Custom KPI (Key Performance Indicator) dashboards
4. AI-generated insights

Growth Dashboard is a self-service, in-house counterpart to the analytics and reporting component of our Digital Marketing service, giving clients a standalone tool for tracking marketing performance across channels.
