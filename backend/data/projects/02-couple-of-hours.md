# Asan Innovators — Case Study: Couple of Hours

Asan Innovators is a technology company headquartered in Nizampet, Hyderabad, Telangana, India. This is one of our 15 portfolio case studies.

## Project Identity

- **Client:** Couple of Hours
- **Client location:** USA
- **Year:** 2024
- **Industry:** Hospitality

## Project Description

We built a per-hour hotel booking platform for Couple of Hours, with an integrated AI chatbot for instant room reservations. This delivered streamlined UX (User Experience) for short-stay hospitality bookings.

## Highlighted Results

- **Per-hour booking model**
- **Instant AI reservations**
- **USA hospitality market reach**

## Technologies and Tools Used

React, Node.js, AI Chatbot, Stripe

## Our Services Reflected in This Project

This project for Couple of Hours drew on multiple of our service disciplines: Web Development (the booking platform, built with React and Node.js), Chatbots (the integrated AI chatbot for reservations), and payment processing via Stripe integration.
