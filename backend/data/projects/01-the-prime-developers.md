# Asan Innovators — Case Study: The Prime Developers

Asan Innovators is a technology company headquartered in Nizampet, Hyderabad, Telangana, India. This is one of our 15 portfolio case studies, and one of our featured "Case Study" write-ups.

## Project Identity

- **Client:** The Prime Developers
- **Client location:** Texas, USA
- **Year:** 2024
- **Industry:** Real Estate
- **Featured status:** Case Study (one of our more detailed featured write-ups)

## Project Description

We built an AI chatbot with RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)-based Large Language Models (LLMs) for intelligent property queries for The Prime Developers. The engagement also included end-to-end workflow automations, a modern website redesign with investment calculators, and full digital marketing.

## Highlighted Results

- **RAG-based AI chatbot** (chatbot capability)
- **End-to-end workflows** (automation capability)
- **Texas, USA market reach**

## Technologies and Tools Used

RAG LLMs, Next.js, Google Ads, Meta Ads, Automation

## Our Services Reflected in This Project

This project for The Prime Developers drew on multiple of our service disciplines: Chatbots (the RAG-based AI chatbot), Automation (end-to-end workflow automations), Web Development (the website redesign, built on Next.js, including investment calculators), and Digital Marketing (full digital marketing, using Google Ads and Meta Ads).
