# Asan Innovators — Case Study: Personiks Cosmetic & Plastic Surgery

Asan Innovators is a technology company headquartered in Nizampet, Hyderabad, Telangana, India. This is one of our 15 portfolio case studies, and one of our featured "Case Study" write-ups.

## Project Identity

- **Client:** Personiks Cosmetic & Plastic Surgery
- **Client location:** Hyderabad
- **Year:** 2024
- **Industry:** Healthcare
- **Featured status:** Case Study (one of our more detailed featured write-ups)

## Project Description

We delivered a full website rebuild on Next.js spanning 200+ pages for Personiks Cosmetic & Plastic Surgery. The engagement also included payment gateway integration, an FAQ chatbot with WhatsApp integration, SEO (Search Engine Optimization) / AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) / GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) optimization, AI voice agents, and a complete digital marketing suite.
