# Asan Innovators — Company Timeline and History

Asan Innovators is a technology company headquartered in Nizampet, Hyderabad, Telangana, India, founded in 2023. Our history and growth is organized into four chapters: Genesis, Momentum, Scale, and Leadership.

## 2023 — Genesis: The Foundation

In 2023, a focused AI-enthusiast team came together at Asan Innovators, exploring tools and modern development workflows. This period laid the intellectual groundwork for the company.

## 2024 — Momentum: Early Growth

In 2024, we began supporting founders and startups, delivering MVPs (Minimum Viable Products), web solutions, and app solutions. Real client outcomes during this period validated our approach and sharpened our focus. All 15 of our documented portfolio case studies are dated to 2024, making it a highly active delivery year.

## 2025 — Scale: Expansion

In 2025, we broadened our service offerings to include AI solutions, workflow automation, web and mobile development, and integrated digital marketing. This expansion moved us from a narrower web/app development focus into our current full six-service model.

## Present — Leadership: At Scale

We're now operating at scale, with:
- 100+ clients across 4+ countries
- A core team of 10+ full-time staff
- A freelancer network of 100+ specialists
- AI products in active development (including LeadPilot)
