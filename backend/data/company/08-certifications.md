# Asan Innovators — Certifications and Recognitions

Asan Innovators is a technology company headquartered in Nizampet, Hyderabad, Telangana, India, founded in 2023. Here are our official certifications and government recognitions, displayed in our website footer under "Certifications & Partnerships — Recognised by national programmes and trusted technology partners."

## Startup India

We're recognized under the **Startup India** initiative, attributed to the Government of India's Ministry of Commerce and Industry, Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade (DPIIT). The badge is displayed with the hashtag "#startupindia."

Startup India is a Government of India flagship initiative intended to build a strong ecosystem for nurturing innovation and startups in the country, driving sustainable economic growth and generating large-scale employment opportunities.

## MSME (Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises) Registration

We're registered as an **MSME (Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises)** entity, attributed to the Ministry of Micro, Small and Medium Enterprises, Government of India.

MSME registration in India is a government classification for micro, small, and medium-sized businesses, which can make registered companies eligible for various government schemes, subsidies, and support programs.

## ISO 9001:2015 Certification

We hold **ISO 9001:2015 Certified Company** status, shown by a "Quality Management — ISO 9001:2015 Certified — Certified Company" badge on our website.

ISO 9001:2015 is an internationally recognized standard for Quality Management Systems (QMS), published by the International Organization for Standardization (ISO). Certification against this standard indicates that a company has implemented a documented quality management system meeting international requirements.

## Where These Certifications Appear

All three certification badges (Startup India, MSME, and ISO 9001:2015) are displayed together in the footer of every page on our website, immediately above the standard site navigation footer, under the heading "Certifications & Partnerships."
